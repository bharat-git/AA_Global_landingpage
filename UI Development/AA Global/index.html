About Us


AA Global is a premium, quality-driven online distributor of global cuisine committed to connecting wholesalers to authentic International food at affordable prices.

Since 2019, AA Global has been delivering authentic global food, health, beauty, books, and lifestyle products to grateful wholesalers, importers, and retail businesses throughout the world. Founded in France and able to offer seamless, fast, and reliable distribution to the United Kingdom and other E.U. countries, AA Global has recently burst onto the global stage by offering the world’s most sought-after and highly rated brands to eager wholesalers throughout the world.

Ideal for business-savvy importers, exporters, wholesalers, and online retailers alike, AA Global connects our customers with phenomenal food, drink, lifestyle, health, wellness, beauty, and educational products that deliver incredible value to an appreciative marketplace. By offering the hottest brands in the most profitable industries online and in a retail setting, AA Global helps our customers maximize profits while thrilling their clients.
