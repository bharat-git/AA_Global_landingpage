About Us


What we're all about

Since 2019, AA Global has been delivering authentic global food, health, beauty, books, and lifestyle products to grateful wholesalers, importers, and retail businesses throughout the world. Founded in France and able to offer seamless, fast, and reliable distribution to the United Kingdom and other E.U. countries, AA Global has recently burst onto the global stage by offering the world’s most sought-after and highly rated brands to eager wholesalers throughout the world.

Ideal for business-savvy importers, exporters, wholesalers, and online retailers alike, AA Global connects our customers with phenomenal food, drink, lifestyle, health, wellness, beauty, and educational products that deliver incredible value to an appreciative marketplace.

Customer Priority

At AA Global, our primary focus is always our customer. We embrace a service-driven approach to business where we believe our customers are placing their trust into our company – and we refuse to let our customers down. AA Global is fully dedicated to going above and beyond for our wholesale, importer, and exporter clients, providing exceptional customer service before, during, and after every sale. AA Global is fully committed to creating an exceptional experience for each of our customers during every single purchase.
